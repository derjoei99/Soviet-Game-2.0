# Soviet Union Game v2.0

An interactive historical game where your decisions shape the fate of Russia and the world from 1917 onward. 

## Live Version
Play the game directly here: [https://derjoei99.github.io/Soviet-Game-2.0/](https://derjoei99.github.io/Soviet-Game-2.0/)

## New in v2.0
- **Randomized Outcomes:** Choices now lead to varied results based on chance.
- **Persistent Gameplay:** Your decisions now have lasting effects on Stability, Ideology, and Economy across chapters.
- **Chapter 2 Unlocked:** Explore the era from 1920 to 1930 (Lenin's Vision and Stalin's Rise).
- **Improved UI:** A cleaner, more thematic design for a better comrade experience.

## How to Play
1. Click **Start Game** from the main menu.
2. Read the historical context and make your choice:
   - **Support:** Pro-Revolutionary path.
   - **Neutral:** Impartial observer.
   - **Oppose:** Resistance or reformist path.
3. Watch your stats evolve and navigate through the chapters of history.
